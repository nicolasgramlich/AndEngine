Detailed Description

The TextureOptions defines with what parameters a ITexture is treated with by the hardware. The MinFilter and MagFilter are responsible for the resulting render-quality. I.e. GLES20#GL_NEAREST will look more pixelated than GLES20#GL_LINEAR, but will render a bit faster. WrapT and WrapS define how TextureCoordinates that exceed the [0,1] limit are treated. I.e. GLES20#GL_REPEAT will repeat the ITexture and GLES20#GL_CLAMP_TO_EDGE will stretch the outermost pixels of the ITexture.
