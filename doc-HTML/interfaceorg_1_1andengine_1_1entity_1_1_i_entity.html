Immediately sorts the IEntitys based on their ZIndex. Sort is stable.

void org.andengine.entity.IEntity.sortChildren ( final boolean  pImmediate)

Sorts the IEntitys based on their ZIndex. Sort is stable. In contrast to IEntity#sortChildren() this method is particularly useful to avoid multiple sorts per frame.

Parameters
pImmediateif true, the sorting is executed immediately. If false the sorting is executed before the next (visible) drawing of the children of this IEntity.
